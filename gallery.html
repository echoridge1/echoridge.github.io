The Journey in Images

About the Photographer

Jason Howard

Jason Howard is a former Royal Marine who discovered the healing power of nature photography after leaving military service. His work focuses on capturing moments of stillness and beauty in the natural world—the same landscapes that became his refuge during difficult times.

Through his lens, Jason seeks to share the therapeutic quality of nature with others who have experienced trauma. Each photograph is an invitation to pause, breathe, and reconnect with the quiet strength found in wild places.

Jason's work has been featured in outdoor and veterans' publications, and he continues to document the journey of building Echo Ridge Retreat—a sanctuary born from his own healing journey.
