The Seven Echo Photographs

Limited edition fine art prints by Jason Howard

Seven photographs that capture moments of stillness, solitude, and quiet strength found in the natural world. Each print is a limited edition of 50, with proceeds supporting Echo Ridge Retreat.

Art That Heals, Stories That Echo

About the Photographer

Jason Howard is a former Royal Marine who discovered photography during his own journey of healing after leaving military service. What began as a way to process his experiences evolved into a profound connection with nature and a medium for helping others.

Jason's work is characterized by patience, presence, and an intimate understanding of light and landscape. He often spends hours—sometimes days—in a single location, waiting for the precise moment when nature reveals its quiet wisdom.

These seven photographs represent pivotal moments in Jason's own healing journey. By sharing them as limited editions, he hopes to extend that healing to others while supporting Echo Ridge Retreat's mission.

"Every photograph is an echo—a reflection of a moment that will never come again. When you hang one of these prints in your space, you're inviting that stillness, that peace, into your daily life."
